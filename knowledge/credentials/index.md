# Registry of graduates

Every credential agun.ai issues is recorded here as an OKF concept and frozen into a hash-stamped
[ledger](../README.md) at prepare time. Each entry is verifiable by its credential id.

- [Cell Optimizer — Agentic Foundations Bronze](cell-optimizer.md)
- [Coverage Analyzer — Agentic Operations Silver](coverage-analyzer.md)
- [Energy Optimizer — Agentic Operations Gold](energy-optimizer.md)

Agents still in training or pending review at agym.ai do not yet appear here; a credential is
created only on graduation.
